Your First Étude

Every musician starts with scales. Every coder starts with practice logs. Your journey begins with a simple three-step process that builds the foundation for everything that follows.

1

Clone the Repository

Start by cloning the hello-orchestra repository. This is your practice space, your blank sheet music, your canvas for learning.

git clone https://github.com/opencodeorchestra/hello-orchestra.git
cd hello-orchestra
2

Create Your Practice Log

Duplicate the PRACTICE_LOG.md template into /practice/yourname.md. This becomes your personal practice journal.

cp PRACTICE_LOG.md practice/yourname.md
3

Make Your First Entry

Fill out one practice log entry. Your first commit is your first note in the orchestra. Document what you learned, what confused you, and what you want to explore next.

git add practice/yourname.md
git commit -m "First practice log entry"

Remember: Your first commit is your first note. Every failure is a lesson. Every log is a score.
